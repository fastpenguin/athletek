About ATHLETEK

Here at Athletek Systems, we strive to use only the highest quality components. Every SportTrack system is hand-built using custom 3d printed parts and uses software written in house. We offer a warranty guaranteeing your SportTrack to be up to our high standards, and are constantly updating our software to bring you the newest features. Our camera tracking system works by using state of the art machine vision technology trained with thousands of hours of video to track your sport games with an industry leading level of accuracy. The system is incredibly portable, being only slightly larger than a hockey puck, yet makes no compromises on quality. The SportTrack comes with everything you need to start filming television quality sports games, just add your phone. It has a built-in battery to keep your phone fully charged, and a super reliable and easy to set up Bluetooth connection for control. The SportTrack is entirely weatherproof; if your players can handle it, we can too.
